About Us

We are a nonprofit organization passionately dedicated to enhancing the safety of neighborhood walkers and joggers. Our mission is simple yet crucial: we strive to promote visibility and safeguard individuals as they navigate their communities. We work hard to reduce accidents and cultivate a culture of awareness on the streets.

Our lightweight vest is designed to make pedestrians more visible to motorists, cyclists, and fellow pedestrians alike. It serves as both a safety measure and a statement, reminding everyone to slow down and pay attention to those sharing the road.

By wearing our vest, individuals can significantly increase their presence, especially during low-light conditions or high-traffic areas. Our commitment to community well-being empowers individuals to take charge of their safety while fostering a sense of unity and responsibility among neighbors. Join us in our journey towards safer streets in our neighborhood.
